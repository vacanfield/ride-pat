U.S. Patents for Other Rotating or Swinging Rides

This page contains the third division of rotating and swinging rides, those that are not similar to carousels or ferris wheels. Multi-axis roundabouts, carousel-ferris wheel combinations, large swings, and large seesaws are included here. Intersecting-path rides and occupant-propelled rides related to flying cages are placed in separate tables on this page. Playground-scale rides are largely excluded.

Other rotating and swinging rides are on the following pages:
Carousels, himalaya rides, circle swings, etc.
Ferris wheels

Miscellaneous rides are on the other rides page or the other amusement devices page.
