U.S. Patents for Ferris Wheels

This page contains the second division of rotating rides, those in which the path of motion is approximately a circle in a vertical plane. Playground-scale rides (including most occupant-propelled rides) are largely excluded. Some rides were identified with the aid of

Norman Anderson, Ferris Wheels: An Illustrated History. (Bowling Green: Bowling Green State University Popular Press, 1992).

This highly-recommended book contains a list of ferris wheel-related patents and shows pages of figures from more than 100 patents.
