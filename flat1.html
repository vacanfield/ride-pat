U.S. Patents for Carousels and Related Rides

This page contains the first division of rotating rides, including tracked roundabouts, in which the path of motion is approximately horizontal and circular. Carousels, circular swings, and himalaya-type rides are included. Occupant-propelled roundabouts are placed in a separate table on this page. Playground-scale rides are largely excluded.
